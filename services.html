⚗️ ETP, STP & RO Plant Maintenance

Our flagship specialisation — we provide complete operations & maintenance support for industrial and municipal water treatment infrastructure, ensuring regulatory compliance and continuous performance.

🏭

Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP)

Primary, secondary & tertiary treatment. Sludge management, chemical dosing, aeration & filter maintenance.

💧

Sewage Treatment Plant (STP)

Biological treatment systems, clarifier & filter maintenance, blower servicing, UV disinfection systems.

🔬

RO Plant (Reverse Osmosis)

Membrane cleaning & replacement, high-pressure pump servicing, CIP systems, water quality monitoring.

📊

Annual Maintenance Contracts

Dedicated AMC packages covering scheduled servicing, emergency support, and performance reporting.
