Our Story

Prakash Deep – Proprietor

Prakash Deep is a seasoned proprietor with over 15 years of hands-on expertise in mechanical, electrical, and civil engineering. Based in Vegivaripalem, Razole Mandal, East Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh, he has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable and cost-effective engineering solutions to industries and institutions across the region.

Specialising in the maintenance of Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP), Sewage Treatment Plants (STP), and Reverse Osmosis (RO) Plants, Prakash Deep ensures that critical water treatment infrastructure operates at peak efficiency. His comprehensive service offerings extend to all associated civil construction and renovation works.

Driven by a commitment to quality, safety, and timely delivery, Prakash Deep has served over 120 clients and completed more than 200 projects, earning long-term trust across industrial, commercial, and municipal sectors.
